Repair desk

Marine repair case register

For underwater services, afloat repairs, shipyard support and technical coordination.

Repair control points

  • Confirm vessel, port, ETA, ETD and alongside or anchorage.
  • Separate firm scope from optional scope.
  • Confirm class attendance, permit, weather and restrictions.
  • Ask written approval before extras.
  • Keep report, photos, CCTV and invoice support ready.

Products desk

Spare parts and product enquiries

For makers, models, part numbers, delivery urgency, quote status and supplier follow-up.

Always confirm

  • Maker, model, part number and required delivery time.
  • Genuine, OEM or high-quality alternative.
  • Quantity, unit, drawings and photos.
  • Delivery port, deadline, packing and courier basis.
  • Commission, payment terms and validity.

Chartering desk

Vessel positions and chartering leads

For offshore support, towage, barges, OSV, PSV, AHTS and project vessel opportunities.

Chartering checks

  • Clarify direct client, contractor or intermediary.
  • Confirm start date, duration, scope and minimum specs.
  • Protect owner and charterer details until needed.
  • Confirm mob, demob, fuel, port costs and payment terms.
  • Do not say fixed before subjects are lifted.
