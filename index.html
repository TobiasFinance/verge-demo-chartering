Deal flow

From enquiry to fixture

  1. Receive enquiry or vessel availability.
  2. Clarify vessel type, location, start date, duration and scope.
  3. Filter sensitive chain details before forwarding anything.
  4. Source suitable units from owners or trusted channels.
  5. Compare suitability, availability, rate, mob/demob and risk.
  6. Present clean offer to client with subjects and validity.
  7. Negotiate rate, period, delivery, fuel, payment and commission.
  8. Issue recap and lift subjects.
  9. Move into CP, operations, delivery, hire and final settlement.

Chain protection

Do not forward

  • Direct owner or contractor contact details
  • End-client identity unless required and approved
  • Broker names inside the chain
  • Internal price assumptions or commission margin
  • Private comments from WhatsApp, email or LinkedIn
  • Documents with signatures, emails, phone numbers or hidden metadata
